B. The visual phase (Phase 0)

New in v2. It's the headline differentiator, so it's worth getting two things straight.

  1. ScopeLüscher & Psycho-Geometrics — credibility and rights.

    Phase 0 is built on the Lüscher Color Test and Psycho-Geometrics (Dellinger). Two flags: (a) both are trademarked, proprietary methods — we should use "inspired-by" colour/shape sets rather than the actual protected materials, to stay clear of licensing issues; and (b) the science behind both is contested, so if a user or journalist asks "is this real psychology?" we need an answer ready.

    Proposed default: implement it as our own "instinctive visual profiling" using inspired-by colours/shapes (not the trademarked palettes), and frame it in the UI as a fun, fast read of instinct — not a clinical diagnosis. Confirm you're comfortable with that framing.

    In plain termsThe colour/shape test you're using comes from named, trademarked systems, and academics argue about whether they actually work. I can absolutely build the experience — I just don't want to copy the protected materials or oversell it as hard science and have it bite us later. My suggestion is to do our own version and present it as "a quick read of your instincts," not a medical test. OK?

  2. BlockingVisual scoring numbers & edge cases.

    The visual step nudges the result with specific weights — colours up to ±15, shape +20, space +6, stimulus +3 — and those can flip a borderline archetype or domain. I need to know: are these calibrated, or placeholder values from the prototype? And what happens when someone picks fewer than 3 colours, picks the "no effect" grey, picks the neutral "Rectangle" shape, or skips Phase 0 altogether?

    Proposed default: Phase 0 is encouraged but optional; if skipped, the Archetype/Domain come from the text quizzes alone. I'll treat the weights as the prototype's values until we have real data to tune them. Confirm.

    In plain termsThe colours and shapes don't just decorate — they actually move the final answer, sometimes enough to change it. My default keeps things working even if they skip it.
